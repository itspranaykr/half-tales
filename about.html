About Half Tales

Half Tales was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to make quality books accessible and affordable to everyone. We believe that books have the power to transform lives, and no one should be deprived of this transformative experience due to high prices.

What started as a small online bookstore has now grown into one of the most trusted platforms for book lovers. We specialize in offering books at half the price of retail stores without compromising on quality.

Our inventory includes everything from academic textbooks to best-selling novels, rare collectibles to the latest releases - all at prices that won't break the bank.

About The Founder

Pranay Kumar

Founder & CEO

"Growing up in a small town with limited access to books, I always dreamed of creating a platform that would make reading affordable for everyone. Half Tales is the realization of that dream."

With a background in engineering and compitative prepration, Pranay started Half Tales from his home with just 100 books. His vision has grown into one of the most beloved affordable book platforms in the region.

Books should enlighten minds, not empty wallets. At Half Tales, we're making reading accessible to all.
